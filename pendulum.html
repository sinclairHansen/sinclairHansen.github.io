Double Pendulum Simulation

Welcome to the Double Pendulum Simulator! This program provides a visual demonstration of key concepts in chaos theory. Each pendulum starts with a slightly different initial angle—theta₁ increases by just 0.01 degrees with each new pendulum. At first, their motion appears synchronized, but over time, their paths diverge dramatically, revealing the sensitive dependence on initial conditions that defines chaotic systems.

Instructions

Use the sliders to adjust the pendulum parameters.

Press the Run button to update the simulation and reflect the changes on the canvas.
