Chaos Theory

Double Pendulum Simulation

Welcome to the Double Pendulum Simulator. This program visualizes one of the core ideas behind chaos theory: tiny differences in initial conditions can lead to dramatically different motion. Each pendulum starts with a slightly different first angle, so their paths may look synchronized at first before eventually separating into different trajectories.
